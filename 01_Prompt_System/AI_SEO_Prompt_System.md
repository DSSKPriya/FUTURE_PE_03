# AI SEO Prompt System

## Project Title

**AI-Powered SEO Content Cluster Strategy for The Brew & Bean Cafe**

---

# Complete AI Prompt

```text
Act as a Senior SEO Content Strategist, Prompt Engineer, and Digital Marketing Consultant with expertise in Search Engine Optimization (SEO), Local SEO, Content Marketing, Keyword Research, and Conversion Copywriting.

Your objective is to generate a complete SEO Content Cluster for a local business website that improves search engine visibility, increases topical authority, enhances user engagement, and follows modern SEO best practices.

Business Details

- Business Name: The Brew & Bean Cafe
- Business Type: Cafe & Bakery
- Location: Koramangala, Bangalore
- Target Audience: Coffee Lovers, Students, Professionals, Families, and Tourists

Your Tasks

1. Conduct comprehensive keyword research by identifying:
   - Primary Keyword
   - Secondary Keywords
   - Long-tail Keywords
   - Local SEO Keywords

2. Classify the search intent for each keyword as:
   - Informational
   - Commercial
   - Navigational
   - Transactional

3. Create one SEO Pillar Blog (2,000–2,500 words) that includes:
   - SEO Title
   - Meta Title
   - Meta Description
   - Introduction
   - H1, H2 & H3 Headings
   - Conclusion
   - Call-to-Action (CTA)
   - Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)

4. Generate five supporting blog articles (800–1,200 words each) targeting related keywords.

5. Develop an Internal Linking Strategy connecting the pillar blog with all supporting blogs.

6. Recommend Local SEO improvements to increase online visibility in Koramangala, Bangalore.

7. Ensure all generated content:
   - Follows Google's EEAT Principles
   - Uses natural keyword placement
   - Maintains professional readability
   - Avoids keyword stuffing
   - Uses a clear heading hierarchy
   - Is optimized for both users and search engines

8. Ensure the content is original, engaging, well-structured, and suitable for publishing on a professional business website.

Output Format

Present the final output in the following order:

- Business Overview
- Keyword Research
- Search Intent Analysis
- SEO Pillar Blog
- Supporting Blog 1
- Supporting Blog 2
- Supporting Blog 3
- Supporting Blog 4
- Supporting Blog 5
- Internal Linking Strategy
- Local SEO Recommendations
- Meta Titles & Meta Descriptions
- Frequently Asked Questions (FAQs)
- Business Call-to-Action
```

---

# Expected Output

The AI is expected to generate the following deliverables:

- Complete Keyword Research

- One SEO-Optimized Pillar Blog
- Five Supporting Blog Articles
- Internal Linking Strategy
- Local SEO Recommendations
- Meta Titles and Meta Descriptions
