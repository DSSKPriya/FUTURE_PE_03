# Internal Linking Strategy

## Overview

This document outlines the internal linking strategy implemented for the **FUTURE_PE_03 – AI SEO Blog & Content Cluster Generator for Business Websites** project.

The project follows a **Pillar Content and Topic Cluster** approach, where one comprehensive pillar blog is supported by five keyword-focused supporting blogs. This structure improves website navigation, enhances user experience, and helps search engines better understand the relationship between related content.

---

# Content Cluster Architecture

The SEO content cluster consists of:

- **1 Pillar Blog**
- **5 Supporting Blogs**

The Pillar Blog serves as the primary content hub, while each Supporting Blog targets a specific long-tail keyword and links back to the Pillar Blog through contextual internal links.

---

# Pillar Blog

## Best Cafe in Koramangala, Bangalore – The Complete Guide to Coffee, Food & Ambience

The Pillar Blog acts as the central resource of the website and provides comprehensive information about **The Brew & Bean Cafe**.

It covers topics such as:

- Specialty Coffee
- Breakfast Menu
- Fresh Bakery Delights
- Comfortable Café Ambience
- Remote Work Facilities
- Customer Experience
- Frequently Asked Questions

The Pillar Blog includes contextual internal links to all five Supporting Blogs, guiding readers to explore specific topics in greater detail while strengthening the overall SEO content cluster.
